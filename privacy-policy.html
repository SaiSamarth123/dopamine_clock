Memento — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026 · Version: 0.0.0.1

Summary

Memento replaces your new tab with a personal accountability dashboard. Settings, mission text, and focus stats stay on your device in Chrome local extension storage. We do not operate accounts, analytics, or a backend that receives your personal data.

Data stored locally

The extension uses chrome.storage.local for birth date, life expectancy, theme and layout preferences, mission and focus statistics, and cached dev.to article metadata. Uninstalling removes this data from your profile.
