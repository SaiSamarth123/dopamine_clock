Memento — Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2026 · Version: 2.0.1

Summary

Memento replaces your new tab with a personal accountability dashboard. Settings, mission text, focus stats, and friction preferences stay on your device in Chrome local extension storage. We do not operate accounts, analytics, or a backend that receives your personal data.

Data stored locally

The extension uses chrome.storage.local for birth date, life expectancy, theme and layout preferences, mission and focus statistics, optional friction host lists, bypass timestamps, and cached dev.to article metadata. Uninstalling removes this data from your profile.

Site friction

When enabled, Declarative Net Request redirects hosts you configure to an in-extension block page. Rules are computed locally; browsing history is not sent to us.

Permissions

  • storage — local settings and timer state
  • declarativeNetRequest — optional site friction
  • alarms — badge and timer completion without a tab open
  • tabs — return to new tab from block page
  • https://dev.to/* — public articles for Intelligence core
